Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with mcp-remote, try clearing the authentication files: 

rm -rf ~/.mcp-auth

For Stripe-related issues, ensure your environment variables are properly set:

  • STRIPE_SECRET_KEY: Your Stripe secret key
  • STRIPE_PRICE_ID: The price ID for your subscription
  • SUCCESS_URL: URL to redirect after successful payment
